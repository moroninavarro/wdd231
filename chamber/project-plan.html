Individual Project Site Plan

SITE NAME

INMOALANIS

I selected this name because its a real name, in my family we have a business with that name. Represents a business that is focused on rent of apartments in Tijuana City, Mexico.

SITE PURPOSE

FOR INMOALANIS

The site provides information about different apartments in Tijuana City, listing images of the apartments and all the related information, such as costs, address, details, etc.

SCENARIOS

FOR INMOALANIS

• Which apartment has the cheapest rent?

• Which apartment has the most safe neighborhood?
